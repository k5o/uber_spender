Uber Spender

Making sense of your Uber ride expense history

IMPORTANT UPDATE:

As of late July 2014, Uber has updated their user dashboard, substantially altering existing methods to fetch trip history. While there may still be a way to gather trip data, most likely what this means is that Uber Spender will be inoperable indefinitely. The bright side is that this extension was partly developed to highlight problems in the previous Uber dashboard, but it looks like they've made a big effort to change that.

Thanks to all who have tried it out - feel free to drop me a line at kevin[at]kokev.in or via twitter @kokev.

=======

Uber's trip history dashboard suspiciously lacks a lot of convenient information, leaving you to wonder how much did I spend on rides this year or how much do I spend per month? Uber Spender is a Chrome Extension designed to help answer those questions.

Download Now for Chrome

Uber Spender takes your Uber trip history data (don't worry, nothing is saved) and packages it into an easy-to-read format, giving you all the information you need. By understanding your ride expense history, you can attempt to limit your spending. Or, just download this so you can boast about your baller habits.

How To Use

  • 1. Log into your Uber Dashboard here: https://clients.uber.com/#!/dashboard
  • 2. Click on the Uber Spender toggle link below the map, just above your trip history table.
  • 3. Magic!
