Uber Spender

Uber's trip history dashboard suspiciously lacks a lot of convenient information, leaving you to wonder how much did I spend on rides this year or how much do I spend per month? Uber Spender is a Chrome Extension designed to help answer those questions.

Download Now for Chrome

Uber Spender takes your trip history data (don't worry, nothing is saved) and packages it into an easy-to-read format, giving you all the information you need. By understanding your ride expense history, you can attempt to limit your spending. Or, just download this so you can boast about your baller habits.


Click to zoom in

How To Use

  • 1. Log into your Uber Dashboard here: https://clients.uber.com/#!/dashboard
  • 2. Click on the Uber Spender toggle link below the map, just above your trip history table.
  • 3. Magic!

    • Potential Issues

  • 1. The extension is reliant on the current version of the Uber Trip History page. I expect Uber to make changes to this page, which may render this extension useless for a short time until an update is pushed. However my hope is that if Uber gets around to changing this page, better stat visualizations will come with it anyway, and our work will be done.
  • 2. The support for non-US currencies may be finicky. Contact me if the maths doesn't add up. It will also always show the $ sign, despite your actual currency.
